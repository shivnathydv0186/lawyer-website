About Sterling & Associates

Excellence in Legal Practice Since 1999

Premier Legal Expertise You Can Trust

For over 25 years, Sterling & Associates has been at the forefront of legal excellence, providing comprehensive legal solutions to individuals, businesses, and corporations across India.

Founded in 1999 by Senior Advocate James Sterling, our firm has grown from a boutique practice to one of the most respected law firms in the region. We pride ourselves on our unwavering commitment to justice, integrity, and client satisfaction.

Our team of experienced attorneys specializes in various practice areas, ensuring that we can handle even the most complex legal challenges with precision and expertise. We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and delivering results that exceed expectations.
