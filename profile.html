James Sterling is the founding partner of Sterling & Associates, bringing over 25 years of distinguished legal experience. A highly respected senior advocate, he has argued landmark cases before the Supreme Court of India and various High Courts.

Known for his meticulous approach and strategic thinking, Mr. Sterling has successfully represented clients in complex criminal, civil, and corporate matters. His commitment to justice and client advocacy has earned him recognition as one of the leading legal minds in the country.
