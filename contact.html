Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I schedule a consultation?

We typically schedule consultations within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, same-day appointments may be available. Call our office directly for immediate scheduling.

What should I bring to my first consultation?

Please bring all relevant documents, any correspondence related to your case, identification, and a list of questions you'd like to discuss. We'll provide a detailed checklist upon scheduling.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer video consultations via Zoom, Google Meet, or phone calls for clients who cannot visit our office in person.

What are your fees?

Our fees vary depending on the complexity of the case and services required. Initial consultations are charged at a fixed rate, and we provide transparent fee structures during your first meeting.

Do you handle cases outside Delhi?

Yes, we represent clients across India and have appeared before courts in multiple states. We also handle international legal matters through our network of associates.

How long does a typical case take?

Case duration varies significantly based on complexity, court schedules, and case type. We provide realistic timelines during consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.
